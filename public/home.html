Why HarkerSG?

HarkerSG is a community where students share their study guides with others. Any member of the Harker School can access the growing collection of study resources for free.
Built with modern web technologies and featuring an intuitive user interface and experience, HarkerSG will reduce your studying stress (even if it's only a little bit). Keep scrolling down to find out more.

Easily select your courses

All of the available courses on HarkerSG are displayed here on the Courses page, including ones for both middle school and upper school. They are conveniently organized by subject so you can easily find the ones you need. Simply select the checkboxes next to the courses you're taking and hit Save to get a personalized feed of study guides.

Quickly search for specific classes

Use the tools at the bottom of the Courses page to filter the course list, revert your changes, or save what you've selected. With the search bar, you can quickly find the course you need or even just display all honors courses. Then, HarkerSG does the work so you don't have to.

Welcome to the Study Guides page

As the primary page of the HarkerSG web app, the Study Guides page was crafted with the goals of students in mind. Think of it as your imaginary older sibling who already went through Harker but has unneeded study guides. Keep scrolling to learn more.

Packed with information and features

Each study guide card features a title, description, icon, date, author, and more. Upon hovering over the study guide, you'll also see options for editing the SG's details, expressing your thanks, reporting the study guide, or moving it to a different folder (coming soon!).

Smart organizational techniques

Each folder on the Study Guides page has its own subcollection of unique study guides that you might only need for a small part of the class. As a result, study guides in folders will only load once you click on it for the first time, which greatly improves loading speed. A clean, simple folder design also leads to an improved user experience.

Find exactly what you need

Again, with searching and sorting tools, you can find the study guides you need with ease. Sort study guides by date, title, or number of likes using the select control. Filter study guides by their titles using the search bar. This all happens instantly, which means no more loading spinners.

Contribute to the collection

Add your own study guides, notes, and folders using the form at the right. Customize all the information that's displayed with your study guide. With real-time notifications for users (coming soon!), this will be the fastest way of sharing your resources with other students.
However, be sure that your study guide does not violate the Harker honor code: that includes past test questions, past graded assignments, and confidential information that's not intended to leave the classroom.
