About HarkerSG

HarkerSG is a free resource where students submit their study guides/notes so that others can benefit from them. HarkerSG was created so you will never have to say "I never knew there was a study guide for this!" again.

Important: Make sure your study guides do not contain previous test questions, graded assignments, or confidential material, as future students may see and use them. By submitting a study guide and checking the box, you ensure that your study guide follows the Harker honor code. I am not responsible for any content that is submitted to this site.

Here is a summary of HarkerSG's best features:
  1. Real-time: Study guides update in real-time, so you NEVER have to reload the page! The "last updated" statistic shows the last time that study guides were modified.
  2. Responsive: This website is mobile-friendly, so you can study on the go. If you have the Google Docs or Quizlet apps downloaded, clicking on study guides will link you directly to their apps.
  3. Secure: All data is kept safe and backed by the security of Google. Spammers beware: your account will be locked.
  4. Customizable: Change color theme settings on the Settings page (by clicking on your name) to personalize your experience.
  5. Fast: HarkerSG pages load quickly because this is a single-page application. Also, study guides in folders won't load until you click on it for the first time, which improves loading speed even more.
  6. Modern: HarkerSG uses modern web technologies and presents a clean user interface in the style of Google material design.

Last but not least, please share HarkerSG with your friends!
